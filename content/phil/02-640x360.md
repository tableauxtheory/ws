"For every pixel displayed on screen, one is taken from the user's mind."

Limiting oneself to 230400 pixels on screen — usually divided into 640x360 for a 16:9 ratio — allows the developer enough room to visually provoke an emotional response in the user, while still preventing over-emphasis on visuals themselves rather than their implications.
